About us 

We are a group of secondary school children trying to change the world for the better. We started this group in 2018 and we hope for it to go far. We are about a group of 10 who plan to make a new law which is that there should be less plastic bottles and more recycling facilities. Do you really want your children and grandchildren to not have te same childhood as you because of plastic?  

What we do

We are trying to reduce plastic waste, especially in schools. 

We are doing this by trying to meet with senior members at our school and possibly with parliament. We hope to change this eventually.
